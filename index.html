Saint Laurent

Saint Laurent usually refers to Yves Saint Laurent, a renowned French fashion designer who revolutionized the industry with his innovative designs and modern approach to haute couture. He founded his eponymous fashion label, Yves Saint Laurent (YSL), in 1961. YSL became known for its iconic pieces such as the tuxedo suit for women, the Mondrian dress, and the safari jacket. Yves Saint Laurent's contributions to fashion continue to influence designers and shape the industry today.
